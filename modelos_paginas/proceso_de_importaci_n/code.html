Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the import process.

How long does the entire process take? expand_more
On average, the process from final selection to delivery at your door takes between 6 to 10 weeks. This includes 2 weeks for inspection and port logistics, 4-6 weeks for ocean transit, and 1-2 weeks for customs clearance and final inland delivery.
Are there any hidden costs? expand_more
Transparency is our core value. Your final quote includes the vehicle cost, shipping, marine insurance, and our service fee. Import duties and local taxes are calculated upfront based on your location so you know the exact landed price before committing.
Can I import a car not in your catalog? expand_more
Yes. We offer a bespoke sourcing service. If you have a specific make, model, or year in mind, our agents in Korea can locate the vehicle, perform a full inspection, and handle the entire import process on your behalf.
What happens if the car is damaged during shipping? expand_more
While our damage rate is 0%, we carry comprehensive marine insurance for every shipment. If any damage occurs, our insurance covers 100% of the repair costs or vehicle value, and our team handles all claim processing.
